Use of Information

The personal information collected through our website is used exclusively for fulfilling your order and creating a personalized letter.

Letter Customization: The details you provide are input into the OpenAI GPT-4 model to generate a customized Santa letter. OpenAI does not store or use this data to train their AI models. You can view their privacy policy here.

Order Fulfillment: Once the letter is created, the letter’s content, along with other customizations (like design choices), is processed by Snipcart for order management. Snipcart's data processing terms are outlined in their Data Processing Addendum. You can view the DPA here.

Payment Processing: Payment details are handled securely by Stripe. We do not store your payment information. You can review Stripe's privacy policy here.

We do not use your data for marketing purposes, nor do we share it with any third parties outside of the requirements necessary to fulfill your order.
